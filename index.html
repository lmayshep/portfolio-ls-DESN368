Lisa Shepherd is a UX and Web Design student at Eastern Washington University (EWU). Originally pursuing her college career as a psychology major, Lisa enjoys getting to the bottom of how people ‘tick’, and has studied human behavior when it comes to technological design. She has a background in illustrating in her free time, as well as creating some short stop-frame animations. She knows how to use programs such as Figma, Photoshop, Fresco, and Illustrator. She also knows HTML, and CSS.
